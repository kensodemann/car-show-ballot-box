Car Shows

The active car show will be displayed at the top. There can only be one active car show. On the backend, attempts to create a second active show should result in an error.

If there is no active car show, a big red button will ask to you create one. Creating one involves giving it a date, perhaps changing the name, and picking the classes. The classes will be initialized with the base classes.

If there is no active car show, the ballot and results pages will be disabled.

Previous finalized car shows display on the first page. Clicking on them gives a full report of the votes.

NOTE: the Results page may be renamed to Classes to avoid confusion.
